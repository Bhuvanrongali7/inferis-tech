FAQ

Common Questions

How long does a website take to build?

Most websites are completed within 2–3 weeks from kickoff. More complex projects with AI integrations may take 3–5 weeks. We'll give you a clear timeline upfront.

Do I own the website after it's built?

Yes — 100%. Once paid, you own all the code, design assets, and content. No lock-in, no licensing fees. It's yours forever.

What if I need changes after launch?

Starter includes 30 days of post-launch support. Growth and Pro plans include ongoing monthly maintenance as part of the retainer.

Can I upgrade my plan later?

Absolutely. You can start with Starter and add AI automation later. We'll just charge the difference and build on what's already there.

Do you require a deposit?

Yes — we require a 50% deposit to kick off the project, with the remaining 50% due on launch day. We also accept payment plans for larger projects.

What platforms do you build on?

We build on plain HTML/CSS/JS, React, or Python depending on your needs. We'll recommend the best fit during your discovery call.
